Careers at Hot Beans - Current Job Openings

At Hot Beans Web, we're always on the lookout for talented individuals who are passionate about web development and eager to make a difference. Below are the current job openings at our company:

Junior Web Developer

Location: Remote

Type: Full-time

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in developing and maintaining web applications.
  • Collaborate with the development team to design and implement new features.
  • Write clean, scalable code following best practices.
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to team knowledge sharing.

Requirements:

  • Basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Familiarity with modern web development frameworks.
  • Good problem-solving skills and a willingness to learn.
  • Relevant degree or completion of a web development course.

Full-Stack Developer

Location: On-site (New York, NY)

Type: Full-time

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain both front-end and back-end components of web applications.
  • Work closely with the UI/UX team to implement design specifications.
  • Integrate third-party APIs and manage databases.
  • Troubleshoot and debug complex issues across the full stack.

Requirements:

  • Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and at least one back-end language (e.g., Python, Node.js).
  • Experience with front-end frameworks like React or Angular.
  • Strong knowledge of database systems (SQL/NoSQL).
  • 3+ years of professional experience in web development.
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.

UI/UX Designer

Location: Remote

Type: Contract

Responsibilities:

  • Create user-centered designs by understanding business requirements and user feedback.
  • Develop wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups.
  • Work closely with developers to ensure accurate implementation of designs.
  • Conduct user research and usability testing to refine and improve designs.

Requirements:

  • Proven experience as a UI/UX Designer with a strong portfolio.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Adobe XD, Figma, or Sketch.
  • Understanding of HTML, CSS, and front-end development is a plus.
  • Excellent communication skills and attention to detail.
  • Relevant degree or certification in Design, HCI, or a related field.

QA Engineer

Location: On-site (San Francisco, CA)

Type: Full-time

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and execute test plans to ensure product quality.
  • Identify, document, and track software defects.
  • Work closely with developers to resolve issues and improve product stability.
