Meet Our Team

At Hot Beans Web, our team is the heart of everything we do. Comprising passionate and talented individuals from diverse backgrounds, we bring together creativity, technical expertise, and a shared vision for crafting outstanding web solutions. Here’s a glimpse into the dynamic team driving our success:

Leon Sjaus - CEO & Founder

Leon founded Hot Beans Web in 2015 with a vision to create a company that blends artistic design with cutting-edge technology. With over 15 years of experience in the web development industry, Leon has led the company from a small startup to a thriving business known for its innovative solutions. As the CEO, Leon oversees the company’s strategic direction, fosters partnerships, and ensures that Hot Beans Web stays ahead in the competitive market. His leadership and passion for technology inspire the entire team.

Emma Martinez - Lead Developer

Emma joined Hot Beans Web shortly after its inception. With a degree in Computer Science and a knack for solving complex problems, she quickly rose to become the Lead Developer. Emma has a deep understanding of both front-end and back-end technologies. Emma leads the development team, guiding projects from initial concept to final implementation. Her expertise in coding standards and best practices ensures that every project meets the highest quality standards.

Sophie Liu - UI/UX Designer

Sophie has a background in graphic design and human-computer interaction. She brings a unique blend of artistic creativity and technical knowledge to the team. Her designs are known for their elegance and user-centric approach. As the UI/UX Designer, Sophie is responsible for creating intuitive and engaging interfaces. She works closely with the development team to ensure that the user experience is seamless across all platforms.

Michael Brown - Project Manager

With a background in business management and a PMP certification, Michael excels in coordinating complex projects. He joined Hot Beans Web to streamline project workflows and improve client communication. Michael oversees project timelines, manages client expectations, and ensures that all projects are delivered on time and within budget. His organizational skills and attention to detail keep the team on track and motivated.

Lena Patel - Marketing Specialist

Lena holds a degree in Marketing and has a keen eye for digital trends. Her experience in digital marketing strategies and content creation makes her a valuable asset to the team. Lena manages the company’s online presence, including social media, SEO, and content marketing. Her efforts help attract new clients and promote the company’s brand effectively.

Carlos Gonzalez - Full-Stack Developer

Carlos is a skilled full-stack developer with a passion for learning new technologies. His background in both front-end and back-end development allows him to work on diverse projects efficiently. Carlos works on developing and maintaining web applications. His versatility and ability to tackle both server-side and client-side challenges make him an integral part of the development team.

Nina Andersen - QA Engineer

Nina has a background in software engineering and quality assurance. Her meticulous nature and attention to detail make her an expert at identifying and resolving issues. As the QA Engineer, Nina is responsible for testing web applications to ensure they are free of bugs and perform flawlessly. Her work guarantees that all products meet the company’s high-quality standards.

Lucas Kim - Junior Developer

Lucas recently graduated with a degree in Web Development and has quickly adapted to the fast-paced environment at Hot Beans Web. His enthusiasm and fresh perspective bring new ideas to the table. As a Junior Developer, Lucas assists with various development tasks and continues to expand his skill set. His eagerness to learn and contribute makes him a promising member of the team.
